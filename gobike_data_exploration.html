Preliminary Wrangling¶

Bay Wheels is a regional public bicycle sharing system in California's San Francisco Bay Area.Bay Wheels is the first regional and large-scale bicycle sharing system deployed in California and on the West Coast of the United States. It was established as Bay Area Bike Share in August 2013. As of January 2018, the Bay Wheels system had over 2,600 bicycles in 262 stations across San Francisco, East Bay and San Jose.The Ford Gobike dataset consists of monthly trip data for 2018. which contains the trip information, in total the dataset contains 1863721 registered trips with 21 variables(duration_sec,start_time,end_time,start_station_id,start_station_name,start_station_latitude,start_station_longitude,end_station_id,end_station_name,end_station_latitude end_station_longitude,bike_id,user_type).

What is the structure of your dataset?¶

The dataset contains 1863721 registered trips with 21 variables(duration_sec,start_time,end_time,start_station_id start_station_name,start_station_latitude,start_station_longitude,end_station_id,end_station_name,end_station_latitude end_station_longitude,bike_id,user_type).The majority of the variables are numeric save for the station names and user types. the main categories of the data set are:

  • Trip Duration:duration_sec,start_time,end_time
  • Station Information: start_station_id,start_station_name,start_station_latitude,start_station_longitude,end_station_id,end_station_name,end_station_latitude end_station_longitude
  • Member Information: user_type

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

What I am most interested in discovering is what variables are best for determining how far a user travels.During what times are bikes most rented? In which months sre there more rentals? Does the fact that a user is a customer or subscriber affect the outcome?

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

I think that the starting and ending station latitude, station_longitude will help to determine how far they travel.The user type will be helpfull to see who the majority of their customer base is.
